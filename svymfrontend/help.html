Help & Support

Welcome to the Tech4Hope Help Center. Here are some frequently asked questions:

How do I apply for a course?

Navigate to the 'Apply for Course' section from the side menu, browse the available courses, and click 'Apply Now' on your desired course.

How can I check my fee details?

Click on 'Fee Details' in the side menu to view your payment history and any outstanding amounts.

What if I forget my password?

On the login page, click 'Forgot Password?' and follow the instructions to answer your security questions and reset your password.

Whom do I contact for technical issues?

Please use the contact details provided in the 'Contact' section for any technical or administrative queries.
